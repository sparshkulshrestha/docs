## **Integration with Popular Version Control Systems**

Firewall Secrets seamlessly integrates with:

* **GitHub**

* **Bitbucket**

* **GitLab**

Supporting multiple version control systems simultaneously, it's adaptable for both small startups and large enterprises. With just one access token (read and comment permissions), you can easily incorporate Firewall Secrets into your existing workflow.

* **Flexible Allowlisting Options**:

<img height="200" src="/allowlist.png" />

* **Version Control System Level**: Apply allowlists within specific VCS platforms.
